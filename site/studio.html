World Lattice

          

            Checks a formula's semantic status across Γ, Γ+φ, and Γ+¬φ worlds.
            A proposition-level independence demonstration — not set-theoretic forcing.
          

          

            
            
            
          

          

        


        

          
Kripke Countermodel Experimental

          

            Bounded finite Kripke countermodel search for intuitionistic propositional logic.
            Finds a world where a formula fails to be forced. Propositional formulas only.
            Bounded search — absence of a countermodel is not a proof of validity.
