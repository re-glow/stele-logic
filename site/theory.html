Curry–Howard

Proof-Term Calculus Experimental

Planned content — Prompt 45 Bidirectional type-checking, β-reduction, de Bruijn indices, FOL fragment (ForallIntro/Elim/ExistsIntro/Elim), term-check and term-normalize CLI commands.
Limitation

Classical proofs are accepted at the rule level. Proof terms are not extracted from classical proofs — acceptance is at rule application, not term level. The classical bridge (Gödel–Gentzen) is formula-level only.

Kripke Countermodels Experimental

Planned content — Prompt 45 Bounded finite Kripke frame search (≤4 worlds default), intuitionistic propositional logic only, absence-of-countermodel caveat.
Important
